    About College

    Bhagwan Mahavir University is a Private University in Surat, Gujarat. The University is recognized by UGC and AIU and it was approved by AICTE, PCI, COA, NCTE, INC.

    The University offers Diploma, UG, PG, Online Programs, Skill Development programs, and Research Programs. Bhagwan Mahavir University takes admission through entrance exams.

    The training department of the university offers a variety of activities such as Personality Development, Verbal Activity Training, etc. The college provides its students with the best faculty and facilities. It has hostels, labs, and laboratories.

    IBM, MRF, Tata Power, Paytm, ICICI Bank, etc are some of the companies that visit colleges every year for the campus selection process.

    Bhagwan Mahavir University Placements

    The College has a dedicated training & placement cell that centrally handles all campus-organized placements. The College organizes different types of seminars to improve the potential and skills of its students.

    The University Training Department carries out a variety of activities such as Personality Development, Verbal Activity Training & Quantitative Aptitude & Logical Reasoning.

    The Average packages offered to the B.tech Computer Science Students is 9.6 lacs per annum.

    Some of the top recruiters of the University are- Dev Krushna Infotech, IBM, Paytm, MRF, Radio Mirchi, ICICI Bank, Reliance Jio Etc.

    Bhagwan Mahavir University Scholarships

    The University offers a number of scholarships to deserving & meritorious students. Below given are the details for the same:

    • Special Need-based Scholarship: This scholarship is provided to students who are from low-income families.
    • Merit-cum means Scholarship: This scholarship is provided to professional & technical courses and the students who got 90% marks in the exam.
    • SC/ST Categories Scholarship: Students who belong to SC/ST categories can avail of the scholarships.

    Apart from this, Various state governments like Mukhyamantri Yuva Swavalamban Yojana and many more scholarships are available.

    How to Avail?

    Candidates must register online as instructed by the university. The student must complete the application form and attach the required documents. After all of the verification is completed, the eligible students will be awarded the amount.

    Bhagwan Mahavir University Facilities

    The BMU ensures a well-maintained Wi-Fi campus along with a wide collection of books and reference materials to meet student’s and faculty's academic and research needs. Some of the services provided by BMU are listed below:

    • The college has laboratories such as a computer lab and a math lab.
    • University having Facilities for indoor games such as Chess, Carrom, Table-tennis, Badminton, And Outdoor games like Football, Hockey, Kabaddi, Volleyball, Cricket & Basketball.
    • The college has fully furnished separate hostels for boys & girls. Both hostels are facilitated by a well-equipped gym.
    • The college has cafeterias for both students and faculty members. All cafeterias serve high-quality food as well as maintain a level of hygiene.
    • Other facilities include Gym, Banking facilities, Medical Facilities, Auditorium, Convenience Store.

    Bhagwan Mahavir University FAQs

    Q. How to apply for admission to Bhagwan Mahavir University?

    Ans: Candidates interested in applying for courses at Bhagwan Mahavir University can apply online on the University website. Candidates must pay the application fees (INR 500) via the online mode of payment after they have completed the application form for the desired course.

    Candidates must register online as instructed by the university. The student must complete the application form and attach the required documents. After all of the verification is completed, the eligible students will be awarded the amount.

    Q. What are the documents required at the time of admission counseling in Bhagwan Mahavir University?

    Ans. The documents required at the time of counseling are 2 sets of self-attested photocopies of S.S.C. Mark sheet, H.S.C. Mark sheet, GUJCET / JEE rank Card, Caste certificate(If applicable), All mark sheet of Bachelor degree and Bachelor degree certificate (provisional) in the relative field, Latest passport size photograph of the candidate.

    Q. Is there any certification program offered by the Bhagwan Mahavir University?

    Ans. Yes, the University offers many certification programs in the field like Entrepreneurship, Child care, Interior Designing, Personality Development, Java, etc.

    Q. How can we reach the Bhagwan Mahavir University?

    Ans. The address for Bhagwan Mahavir University is Sr. No. 149, VIP Road, Bharthana Road, Vesu, Surat, Gujarat 395007.
